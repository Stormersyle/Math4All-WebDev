Democratizing Competitive Math

Our Mission

Inequity in education is one of the major problems in today’s society. Students from low-income families and marginalized communities are often unable to pursue their passions and explore their intellectual interests, due to the lack of opportunity and resources; this is especially the case in the field of mathematics, where many books/classes are very expensive. At Math4all, we combat this problem head-on by providing free, accessible math education to these students, along with individualized support to help them fully realize their potential in mathematics. Our belief is that everyone deserves to have the opportunity to pursue their passion for math, regardless of their family’s socioeconomic status, the color of their skin, or the location in which they live. At Math4All, our mission is to make this a reality.

Our Community

We bring together students from all over the world to create a welcoming community for middle and high school students who love math. Our aim is to connect students who don’t have access to local competitive math communities with other students who are similarly passionate about math.

Our Impact

With over 1000 students in our community and $110,000 given in prizes, Math4All aims to bring competitive math to every indvidiual part of over 25 countries across the world.

High Quality Classes

Over 40 qualified staff members work together to create contest problems, class material, and help students throughout our network. We bring free classes to students throughout the world.
