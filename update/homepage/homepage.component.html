Math4All
Democratizing Competition Math
Mission
Math4All is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to promoting math/problem solving and making mathematics more accessible. By offering free, high-quality contests and classes, we aim to level the playing field in competitive math for underprivileged students who canâ€™t afford expensive textbooks/tutoring, as well as foster a love for math and problem solving in many students as possible.
Community
Our events are able to reach over 1000 indviduals globally, over 800 of whom are in our discord server. We have over 40 experienced student volunteers who have or continue tohelp with Math4All events. We are extremely proud of every single member that contributes, either by planning or attending our events. We have reached students from over 25 countries, ranging from the US to Malaysia.
Classes
Math4All offers free, high quality live classeses meant to prepare for competition math. High school students individually create courses about math topics in their expertise. Classes occur each year during the fall and spring sessions. Check out the classes for this year!
Contests
Throughout the year, Math4All staff members create contest math exams which are all individually taken during the contest time period. These free-to-attend contests are meant to give members in our community to gain some practice and reward the members with top scores. Check out the competitions from previous years!
Awards
There is no competition without winning prizes. Math4All awards members in our community with the gracious sponsorships we recieve. Math4All has given out over $110,000 to students in our community ranging from notebooks, AoPS coupons, Gift Cards and participation awards.
Sponsors
The majority of Math4All runs on the help of volunteers and sponsorships. Financial donation to Math4All allows us to comeback every year to provide awards to individuals in our community. Since Math4All is also PVSA-certifying, students volunteer can recieve service hours for their voluntary contribution to our community. All of the money donated to our foundation will be directed towards our cause. All donations to our cause are also tax-deductible.
